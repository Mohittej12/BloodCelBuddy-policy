Privacy Policy

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. BloodCelBuddy does not collect or store personal data unless explicitly stated and permitted. We only collect the minimum data required for the app to function and to improve the service.

Information We Collect

To use BloodCelBuddy you create an account with an email address and password; these are used only for authentication. In addition, you may upload images for analysis. We collect only the information required for these purposes:

  • Account Credentials: Email and password for authentication. Passwords are stored only in hashed form.
  • Images: Images you choose to analyze. These are processed to produce the analysis output you see in the app.
  • Optional Analytics: Minimal, aggregated app usage data (crash reports, feature usage) to improve performance — these are non-identifying.

Note: We do not collect contact lists, address books, or device identifiers unless you explicitly provide them and permit storage.

How We Use Data

Data collected is used only to:

  • Authenticate your account and manage access (email + password).
  • Analyze images you upload and provide the analysis output to you.
  • Improve app performance and features using aggregated, anonymized analytics.
  • Respond to support or account requests when you contact us.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing. Any third-party services used (for example, crash-reporting) will receive only minimal, non-personally-identifying information and are subject to their own privacy terms.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain only the data necessary for the app to operate. Specifically, we store your email and a hashed version of your password for authentication, and any images you upload for analysis (only if you choose to upload them). If you request deletion, we will remove your stored data from our servers within 7 business days where applicable.

To request deletion or for privacy questions, email us at bloodcellbuddy@gmail.com.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access. Passwords are stored using secure hashing; images and other stored data are protected with appropriate access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Please contact us if you suspect a breach.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will post any changes here with an updated date. Continued use of BloodCelBuddy after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
